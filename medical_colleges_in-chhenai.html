top 5 medical college

1. Madras Medical College (MMC)

 Madras Medical College (MMC), established in 1835, is one of the oldest and most reputed medical colleges
in India. It is a government-run institution affiliated with The Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University. 
MMC is known for its high-quality education, experienced faculty, and excellent clinical exposure through the Rajiv Gandhi Government General Hospital.

Cutoff required (approx):
NEET UG: 660–700 marks for MBBS under General Category

Address:
Madras Medical College,
E.V.R. Salai, Park Town,
Chennai – 600003, Tamil Nadu.

Fees:
Government quota: ₹13,610 per year
Management quota: As per TN government norms.

Courses offered:
MBBS, MD, MS, DM, MCh, and various diploma courses.

Key Features:
• One of India’s oldest medical institutions.
• Excellent hospital facilities and patient exposure.
• Highly reputed for postgraduate and specialty training.

2. Stanley Medical College (SMC)

 Stanley Medical College (SMC), established in 1938, is a government medical institution
 affiliated with The Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University. The associated Government Stanley Hospital is one of the largest multi-specialty 
 hospitals in South India, offering students vast clinical experience.

Cutoff required (approx):
NEET UG: 640–690 marks for MBBS under General Category

Address:
Stanley Medical College, Old Jail Road,
Royapuram, Chennai – 600001.

Fees:
Approx. ₹13,000 per year under government quota.

Courses offered:
MBBS, MD, MS, DM, MCh, and PG Diploma programs.

Key Features:
• Famous for its Department of Plastic Surgery and Gastroenterology.
• Excellent clinical exposure and infrastructure.
• Renowned for research and patient care.

3. SRM Medical College Hospital and Research Centre

 SRM Medical College Hospital and Research Centre is part of SRM Institute of Science and 
Technology, a deemed-to-be university. Established in 2005, it offers world-class infrastructure, advanced medical equipment, and excellent academic facilities.

Cutoff required (approx):
NEET UG: 400–500 marks (varies by category and quota)

Address:
SRM Nagar, Potheri, Kattankulathur,
Chengalpattu District, Chennai – 603203.

Fees:
Approx. ₹22,50,000 per year for MBBS.

Courses offered:
MBBS, MD, MS, DM, MCh, and Ph.D. programs.

Key Features:
• Modern infrastructure and hospital with 1500+ beds.
• Excellent placement and internship support.
• Research-oriented programs with global collaborations.

4. Saveetha Medical College and Hospital

 Saveetha Medical College and Hospital,
 established in 2008, is part of Saveetha Institute of Medical and Technical Sciences (Deemed University).
  It is known for its advanced facilities and strong focus on research, innovation, and clinical training.

Cutoff required (approx):
NEET UG: 400–550 marks depending on category.

Address:
Saveetha Nagar, Thandalam,
Chennai – 602105, Tamil Nadu.

Fees:
₹25,00,000 per year (approx.) for MBBS.

Courses offered:
MBBS, MD, MS, DM, MCh, and B.Sc. Nursing.

Key Features:
• Modern hospital with over 1200 beds.
• Strong emphasis on research and global exposure.
• Recognized by NMC and listed in WHO directories.

5. Sri Ramachandra Institute of Higher Education and Research (SRIHER)

 Sri Ramachandra Institute of Higher Education and Research (SRIHER),
 formerly known as Sri Ramachandra Medical College and Research Institute, is a leading private medical university in Chennai. It was established in 1985 and is
  recognized by the National Medical Commission (NMC).

Cutoff required (approx):
NEET UG: 480–550 marks for MBBS

Address:
No.1, Ramachandra Nagar, Porur,
Chennai – 600116, Tamil Nadu.

Fees:
₹25,00,000 – ₹27,00,000 per year for MBBS.

Courses offered:
MBBS, BDS, MD, MS, DM, MCh, M.Sc., B.Sc. Nursing, and other allied health programs.

Key Features:
• Deemed university with modern hospital facilities.
• International partnerships and research opportunities.
• Excellent placement and internship programs.
